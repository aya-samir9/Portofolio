About Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Liverpool and the Egypt national team. He is known for his speed, dribbling, and goal-scoring ability. Born on June 15, 1992, in Nagrig, Egypt, Salah began his professional career at El Mokawloon before moving to Basel, where his European career began.

Achievements

  • Premier League Golden Boot (2018, 2019)
  • UEFA Champions League Winner (2019)
  • Africa Cup of Nations Top Scorer (2017)
  • FIFA Club World Cup Winner (2019)
